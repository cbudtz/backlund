Consulting

BACKLUND Consulting as an advicer regarding Alternatives to Conventional Wastewater Treatment - Alternatives aiming at Closing The Loop in Sanitation regarding Water & Nutrients using among others Diverting Toilet Systems & Waterless Urinals and Zero Discharge Willow Evapotranspiration Systems. Advice regarding Multifunctional Short Rotation Willow Plantations Irrigated with Pre Treated Wastewater and Sludge - Combining Biomass Energy Production and Phytoremediation of Cadmium in Soils improving Soil and Products Quality in Agriculture - is also an important Consulting Area.

BACKLUND has Expert Knowledge regarding Sustainable Sanitary Solutions and has participated in many project giving advice to Environmental Protection Agencies, Counties, Municipalities, Organizations, Consultants and Individuals. We among others assist:

  • Consultants and Engineering Companies with Special Knowledge in Projects
  • Authorities Dveloping Competences regarding Sustainable Sanitary Solutions for Rural Areas, Eco Villages, Summer Cottages, Allotment Gardens and other Off Grid Living
  • Aid Organizations by supplying Special Knowledge and Experience to the Planning and Implementation of Sanitary Projects in Developing Countries
  • Schools and Universities by assisting in Planning of and lecturing in Metablic Rift & Matabolic Shift related to Food, Water & Nutrients Streams related to Sanitation
  • Sanitation Customers Selecting System, Technology and Components  
  • Producers trying to Develope & Market Sustainable Systems
